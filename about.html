Appu Browser Extension

Reduce personal information footprint across web. In this first release, Appu tries to prevent
password reuse across websites by warning users about it. You can read about why reusing passwords
is bad here. For bugs, suggestions or more information, shoot an email to "yhm@cc.gatech.edu" with subject "[Appu]".
